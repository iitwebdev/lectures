IRS recognition of tax exempt status

The PSF is a non-profit, tax-exempt 501(c)(3) corporation.

  • Final determination letter from the IRS, May 5, 2006 (960KB PDF scan)
  • Advanced ruling exemption letter, Feb. 25, 2003 (OCR of original)

The original copies of our tax exempt application, IRS form 1023, have been lost by the PSF and the IRS. Thus, we are not able to provide copies of them. The IRS sent us this letter to explain.
